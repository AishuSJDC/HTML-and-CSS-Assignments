Study Essentials Stationery List:

  • Notebooks (ruled, spiral-bound)
  • Loose-leaf paper
  • Pens (black, blue, red)
  • Pencils (mechanical or traditional)
  • Highlighters (assorted colors)
  • Erasers
  • Correction tape or fluid
  • Pencil case or organizer
  • Sticky notes (various sizes and colors)
  • Index cards
  • Binder clips or paper clips
  • Stapler and staples
  • Ruler
  • Calculator (scientific or graphing, depending on needs)
  • USB flash drive or external hard drive for digital storage
  • Whiteboard and markers (for brainstorming or study groups)
  • Post-it tabs or flags for bookmarking pages
  • Planner or agenda to keep track of assignments and deadlines
  • Desk organizer or caddy for storing stationery neatly
  • Backpack or tote bag to carry all supplies conveniently
